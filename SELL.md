# VendorPulse — Sales Guide

## Ideal Customer Profile

- **Company size**: 100–5,000 employees
- **Industry**: Manufacturing, Healthcare, Technology, Financial Services, Retail
- **Budget**: $50K–$500K annual procurement/vendor spend
- **Pain points**: Spreadsheet-based vendor tracking, no visibility into vendor risk, manual scorecard processes

## Value Proposition

VendorPulse replaces spreadsheets and fragmented tools with a single platform that gives procurement teams complete visibility into their vendor relationships, spend patterns, and risk exposure.

4. **Compliance ready** — Full audit trail for SOX, ISO 27001, and procurement audits

## Objection Handling

**"We already use spreadsheets"**
> Spreadsheets can't alert you when a vendor''s financial health deteriorates, track contract auto-renewals, or give you a real-time risk score. VendorPulse takes 30 minutes to set up and starts adding value immediately.

**"Our ERP handles vendor management"**
> ERPs track transactions, not relationships. VendorPulse layers AI-powered relationship intelligence on top of your existing spend data via CSV import or API.

**"Too expensive"**
> At $89/month, VendorPulse costs less than one hour of a consultant''s time. One prevented vendor incident or renegotiated contract typically pays for years of the platform.
