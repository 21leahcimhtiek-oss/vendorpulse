# VendorPulse — Product Description

## One-liner
**Manage every vendor relationship with precision.** AI-powered vendor intelligence for procurement teams.

## Short Description (150 words)
VendorPulse is the modern procurement intelligence platform that gives enterprise teams complete visibility into their vendor ecosystem. Track vendor performance with customizable scorecards, analyze spend patterns across departments and categories, and let AI automatically detect risk signals before they become incidents.

Unlike bloated legacy tools that take months to implement, VendorPulse connects in hours and starts delivering value immediately. Import your existing spend data via CSV, onboard your vendors, and get your first AI risk assessment in minutes.

Built for procurement managers, CFOs, and operations teams who are tired of managing vendors in spreadsheets.

## Feature Highlights

### 🎯 Vendor Scorecards
Rate every vendor across four dimensions — Quality, Delivery, Communication, and Value. Track performance trends over time and identify your top performers and underperformers with a single dashboard view.

### 🛡️ AI Risk Monitoring
Our GPT-4o powered risk engine analyzes vendor profiles, historical spend, and performance data to generate risk scores (0-100) and identify specific risk factors. Get automatic alerts for financial, compliance, operational, and reputational risks.

### 📊 Spend Analytics
Visualize your procurement spend by vendor, category, department, or time period. Import records from any ERP via CSV and uncover spending patterns, cost optimization opportunities, and budget overruns.

### 📄 Contract Tracking
Never miss a renewal. Track all vendor contracts in one place — view expiration dates, auto-renewal flags, and contract values. Get proactive alerts 30, 60, and 90 days before contracts expire.

### 🔔 Smart Alerts
Configurable notifications for risk events, contract expirations, spend anomalies, and vendor status changes. Stay on top of your vendor relationships without manual monitoring.

## Use Cases

1. **Vendor qualification** — Score and tier new vendors before engagement
2. **Quarterly business reviews** — Use scorecard data to drive vendor conversations
3. **Risk audits** — Generate a complete risk picture for compliance or board reporting
4. **Budget planning** — Analyze YTD spend by category and department
5. **Contract renewal** — Identify contracts expiring in the next 90 days

- **VP of Procurement** — Needs portfolio visibility and risk oversight
- **Procurement Manager** — Day-to-day vendor relationship management
- **CFO / Finance Director** — Spend analytics and cost control
- **Operations Manager** — Vendor performance and SLA tracking
